Easy Gluten-Free Cinnamon Rolls

An easy, delicious gluten-free recipe made from gluten-free bread mix. You can alter the amount of whipping cream and glaze depending on how decadent you want them!

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 (20 ounce) package gluten-free bread flour mix (such as Bob's Red Mill®)
  • 2 eggs, at room temperature
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tablespoon milk

Steps

    Step 1
  1. Mix warm water and yeast packet from bread mix together in a bowl and let stand until foamy, about 5 minutes.
    2. Step 2
  2. Place bread flour mix in the bowl of a stand mixer and add eggs, vegetable oil, vinegar, and yeast mixture; mix using the stand mixer set to low speed until smooth. Turn speed to medium and beat until mixture thickens, about 15 seconds. Transfer dough to a greased bowl, cover bowl with a dish, and allow dough to rise in a warm place for 45 minutes.
    3. Step 3
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    4. Step 4
  4. Spread dough onto a piece of plastic wrap. Brush melted butter over dough and sprinkle white sugar and cinnamon over dough.
    5. Step 5
  5. Roll dough into a jelly roll-type loaf by lifting 1 end of the plastic wrap and forming dough into roll. Place roll into a glass baking dish. Cut 1/2- to 1-inch slits into the loaf. Pour cream over the loaf.
    6. Step 6
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until dough is cooked through, 20 to 30 minutes. Break rolls apart.
    7. Step 7
  7. Mix confectioners' sugar and milk together in a bowl until glaze is smooth; drizzle over warm rolls.
